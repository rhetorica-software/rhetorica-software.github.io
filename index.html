Customisable OSINT Workspaces

Griffin offers a range of dedicated workspaces, built specifically to support users with their OSINT analysis.

Griffin offers everything from a browser only workspace, through to a fully fledged OSINT desktop with pre-installed tools and a built-in password manager. The workspaces can be customised to suit your agencies unique needs and requirements.

These workspaces are accessed directly through your normal work internet browser, meaning you don't need a separate computer. Griffin workspaces support audio, microphones and web cameras, and users can freely upload and download files between the workspace and their laptop.
